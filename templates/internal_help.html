Password reset
Call extension 201 on the internal helpdesk line via SIP to receive a temporary password. Identify yourself by username after the tone.
Use a SIP client such as baresip or pjsua. The PBX is reachable externally on port 5060/UDP.
Alternatively, submit a ticket at: http://tickets.railinfra.local/reset (offline)
